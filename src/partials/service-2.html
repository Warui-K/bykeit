2. Mail Management

Tailored for corporates that require mail delivered or picked up. We base this service on the fact that most important transactions often require the exchange of hard copy documents;

e.g a bank opening exercise will eventually require the account owner to collect their cheque book from the bank or a contract negotiation will lead to the exchange of the contractual document for execution. These are just 2 of the many business transactions that exist.

a). We create a system that enables our clients trace the chain of custody of their mail and ensure accurate and timely deliveries.

b). We pick up and drop off documents at designated places and where need be, our riders will wait for return copies. We ensure that we have a signed record that the documents were picked up and dropped off as instructed, for accountability purposes.
